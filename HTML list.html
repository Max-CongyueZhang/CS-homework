html basics

""is used to replace less than sign and greater than sign.

The basic format of html is like following:
"!DOCTYPE html"
"html"
"head"
"meta charset="utf-8""
"title" "/title"
"/head"
"body"
"/body"
"/html"

"h1" "/h1" is used for title.

"h2" "/h2" is used for subtitles.

"p" "/p" is used for paragraph.

"br" is used for changing to next row.

emphasis

"em" "/em" is used for italic.

"strong "/strong" is used for bold.

lists

"ul" "/ul" is used for unordered list.

"ol" "/ol" is used for ordered list.
